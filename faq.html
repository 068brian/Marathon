Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the Nairobi Marathon?

You can register online through our official website by visiting the Register page.

What are the different race categories?

We offer three categories: 10K Run, Half Marathon (21K), and Full Marathon (42K).

Can I volunteer for the event?

Yes, we welcome volunteers. Please contact us for more details.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, the registration fee varies depending on the race category you choose.

What should I bring on race day?

Bring your registration confirmation, ID, running gear, and any essentials you may need.

Are there age restrictions for participation?

Yes, participants must be at least 16 years old for the Half and Full Marathon categories.

Can I transfer my registration to another person?

No, registrations are non-transferable.
